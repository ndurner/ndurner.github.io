Kimi K3, the newest model from Chinese lab Moonshot AI, has been released ([blog post](https://www.kimi.com/blog/kimi-k3)). It’s a big model - 2.8T parameters.

Artificial Analysis has it at GPT-5.6 Terra/Sol capability level, but it’s also priced that way - making it the most expensive Chinese model:

The only reasoning mode currently offered is “max”, with additional reasoning modes planned. Accordingly, Simon Willison’s Pelican test [cost him 25 cents](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jul/16/kimi-k3/) with K3 (GPT-5.6 Sol @medium cost him 10.55 cents, @max cost him 48.55 cents).
