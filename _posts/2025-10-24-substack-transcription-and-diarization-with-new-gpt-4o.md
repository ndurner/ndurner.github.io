OpenAI have released a new speech-to-text model that also supports diarization (speaker separation and attribution): [GPT-4o Transcribe Diarize (aka “gpt-4o-transcribe-diarize”)](https://platform.openai.com/docs/models/gpt-4o-transcribe-diarize). It’s priced the same (estimated) cost of regular gpt-o4-transcribe and Whisper. Immediate findings:

- chunk limit of 1400 seconds
